Beans on Toast

Description

A traditional british dish, simple to make, incredibly tasty and very cheap

Ingredients

  • 1 x Tin of baked beans
  • 2 x Slices of bread
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Your cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Empty the tin of beans into a saucepan and turn the heat to a medium setting and allow it to simmer for a few minutes
  2. Place your bread into a toaster and turn it on
  3. Grate the cheese
  4. Once the toast has finished, spread the butter/margarine on top
  5. Take the now heated beans over the top of the nicely buttered toast (The toast wouild ideally be on a plate of some kind)
  6. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the top
  7. Enjoy!!!
