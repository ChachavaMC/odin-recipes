Cheese Sandwich

Description

Another incredibly simple to make "meal", very cheap, very tasty and very low effort

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter or margarine
  • Your cheese of choice
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Slice the cheese
  3. slice the tomatoes
  4. Place the sliced cheese and tomatoes on top of once slice of bread
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top of the cheese/tomato (butter side down)
  6. Enjoy!!!
